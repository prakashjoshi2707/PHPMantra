46th Annual Cultural Competition

It’s now your chance to bring out your excellence is recitation, essay writing, elocution, quiz and debate at the 46th Annual Cultural Competition conducted by Ramakrishna Mission Shillong.

The programme is for the students of Meghalaya - from nursery to the college level. It aims to provide the budding youth a platform to show-case their talent and instil confidence in them.

There is no entry fee for the Cultural Competition, but you can always extend your helping hand for the future citizens of India!

Rules and regulations

  • General Information

    1. The competition is open for participants from the state of Meghalaya only.
    2. Enrolment has to be made online only. It can be done through a smart phone also. Request, please enroll by 31st July 2020.
    3. If necessary, for enrolment or for uploading of video help from facilitation centre at Ramakrishna Mission, Vivekananda Cultural Centre could be availed.
    4. Participant will make a video of his/her performance and upload it in the given link. Video should be recorded against a plain background - a single color wall with no photo, motifs or wall hangings etc.
    5. Participants must make the video recording through a mobile phone/ tablet and send in https://acc.rkmshillong.org or send to WhatsApp no._______
    6. Date of live streaming of competition through YouTube and Facebook will be intimated through text message/mail.
    7. Result of each competition will be available only in the website after the competition is over.

  • Recitation 

    1. Nursery and KG participants may recite any nursery rhyme of his or her choice.
    2. Participants from class -I (one) will have to recite the selected poem, which can be downloaded from the website www.rkmshillong.org or from the given link.
    3. For Recitation, Chanting/Singing in tune shall not be allowed.

  • Elocution

    Participant must speak on the given Topic and record the video of his speech in standing position for maximum four minutes. Reading from a script will disqualify the candidature. Video must be made in one shot.

  • Essay

    Essay has to be written within 1000 words and written in hand and scanned copy of the same should be uploaded by 6.00 P.M. of 5th August 2020. No typed copy or uploaded after the cut-off date shall be accepted. Handwriting should be neat.

  • Debate

    1. Participants to join as a team of two members, one to speak ‘for’ the motion and the other ‘against’ the motion.
    2. Competition will be held on the given topic.
    3. Debate will be held on 30th August and/or 5th September from 2 P.M.
    4. Based on the number of participants a Preliminary round of Debate may be conducted on 30th August and the final competition will be held on the 5th September from 2 P.M. Participants will be intimated about the other formalities indue course. Both preliminary and final will be held on different topics.
    5. The topic for preliminary round, and final is as given below:
      1. For Preliminary Round (If required): In the opinion of the House, “We are the Cave-man of 21st Century”.
      2. Final round – In the opinion of the House “Atmanirbhar Bharat” is a utopia
    6. Entire competition will be conducted online and participants shall have to have fast internet and to use a devise having a web camera to join from their home.
    7. Reporting will commence one hour before the start of the competition. Competition will be streamed live on YouTube and Facebook.

  • Quiz

    1. Participants are to submit their names in pairs as a team.
    2. Preliminary round will be held online on 30th August at 11 A.M. Each team shall have to have access to fast internet and will appear from a common device having a web camera. Movement of the participants shall be noticed and candidature may be cancelled in doubtful cases, like found using google search etc. Microphone of the device shall not be muted until asked.
    3. Reporting of the teams will start one hour before commencement of the online test. IN NO CASE LATE REPORTING OR REPORTING AFTER COMMENCEMENT OF THE COMPETITION SHALL BE ENTERTAINED.
    4. Modalities and other Rules for the online test will be announced on the day of the competition.
    5. Semi-final and Final round will be oral and held at the premises of Ramakrishna Mission Vivekananda Cultural Centre, Quinton Road, Shillong-1. Besides the participants, other accompaniments may not be allowed inside the competition venue. Semis and Final also will be streamed in the YouTube and Facebook.
    6. Detailed procedure to be adopted for reporting etc. will be intimated in due course.
    7. Topics: Swami Vivekananda, General History, Geography, Science, Current affairs, Sports, Environment & North East.
