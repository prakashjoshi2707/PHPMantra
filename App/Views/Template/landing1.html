About Annual Cultural Competition

To encourage the inherents of children, various competitions like Drawing and Painting, Recitation (in English, Hindi, Nepali Bengali and Sanskrit), Debate, Essay, Quiz etc. are organized for Nursery to College-level students. About 3,000 competitors vie for the desired recognitin in various categories every year.
